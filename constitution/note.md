perception 
can percieve grids, shapes and objects and languages. does not reason or do anything else, only percieves the data being given to it 

cue encoding 
takes the percieved notes and converts them into cues that can activate traces. if for example the system percieves quantum computing, it would activate quantum, computing and quantum computing traces

dmf
system that activates traces based on what cues are present. certain cues activate certain traces via learnt relationships. no logic is done here, only activation. 


binding
understands/interprets what the input is asking/talking about and relates to context, etc. helps pick the right basin along with trace activation 

contextop 
converts the question into different hypothesis/candidate_frames. each of these are now weighed on their merits and activations 

interference
if there are certain traces that contradict each other or raise issues, they increase instability within the candidate frames. 

basins 
learned system/energy gradient that activates based on trace activation and binding and interference. multiple basins may be activated and the ones that survive and cross a threshold energy are decided to be represented 
also contain quantized memory stores with knowledge that are only activated after the basins have been activated.

lucidity. 
the part that collapses the multi hypothesis system into one or few hypothesis merged into one final output. also emits interpretation on what answer is required 

decoder
converts final committed lucidity state into fluent language
